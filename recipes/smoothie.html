strawberry-banana-breeze-smoothie

Perfect smoothie for warm summer days

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup Almond Breeze Original or Unsweetened Original almondmilk
  • 1 cup fresh or juice packed pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon slivered or sliced almonds (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon flax seeds (Optional)

How to make

  1. Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
  2. Drink it
  3. Feel Happy!
